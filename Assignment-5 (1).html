c) Why is this a situation where contrasts would be helpful? Explain briefly.

This is a situation where contrasts would be helpful because the researcher is not interested in all the comparisons between groups, but rather in two specific ones. The two specific hypotheses were: whether the average of the two treatments is different from the control, and whether the two treatments differ from one another.
