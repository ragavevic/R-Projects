b) Briefly explain why MANOVA is something we would consider to analyze these data.

There are three response variables, useful, difficulty and importane, rather than just one. In order to do a test with more than one response variable, we need to use multivariate ANOVA rather than regular ANOVA.

c) Make side-by-side boxplots of each of the three explanatory variables for each of the three groups. For full credit, do this in one ggplot.

d) What does your plot suggest to you about the research objectives mentioned in the opening paragraph? Explain briefly.

Website are generally highest in all three explanatory variables. Nurse and video are about the same for difficulty and useful. In importance the nurse group is higher than video. Some of the groups appear to be different on some of the variables, which would make a MANOVA come out significant.
